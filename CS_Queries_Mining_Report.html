Detailed Theme Insights

4.1 Address Management
9,845 tickets
Address validation and delivery address updates represent the largest category of customer support inquiries. Customers struggle with address input during checkout, autocomplete functionality failures, and subsequent address modifications. This friction directly impacts order fulfillment and customer satisfaction.
Customer Feedback
"The system seems unable to accept a street address that's been perfectly valid for years. I've tried multiple times and keep getting error messages."
"When I type in the address it doesn't autocomplete properly. I have to manually enter everything which takes much longer and feels error-prone."
4.2 Pause / Cancel Subscription
5,218 tickets (combined pause/cancel from theme breakdown)
Subscription management is a critical pain point with 6,049 total tickets. Customers frequently need to pause or cancel subscriptions but struggle to find these features or complete the process independently. This includes requests to modify delivery frequency and preferences.
Customer Feedback
"I placed my first subscription order and now I would like to pause my subscription while I'm on holiday. I can't find where to do this in the app."
"Good day I am away next week. Please no UCOOK delivery next week. Can I pause it?"
4.3 Delivery & Logistics
3,922 tickets
Delivery issues comprise the third-largest support category. Customers struggle with missing delivery notifications, inability to request specific delivery times, and lost or missing packages. These issues impact trust and satisfaction significantly.
Customer Feedback
"I haven't received any notification that my order is out for delivery today. How am I supposed to know when it's arriving?"
"Can courier please deliver parcel after 12h00? I won't be home in the morning."
4.4 Billing & Payment
3,169 tickets
Payment and billing issues directly impact customer trust and retention. Customers report incorrect charges, payment method failures, and refund complications. These issues require immediate attention as they affect financial relationships with customers.
Customer Feedback
"I've made payment on 13 April but I've not received my box yet. When will it arrive? The charge appears twice on my statement."
"The amount shown for my subscription is definitely incorrect. I was charged far more than the advertised price."
4.5 Product & Quality
848 tickets
While lower in volume than other categories, product quality issues are critical for brand reputation. Customers report spoiled or damaged items, missing ingredients, and quality complaints that directly impact the core value proposition of the service.
Customer Feedback
"I made the mussels last night and the one onion and red pepper was rotten. This component needs better quality control before shipping."
"My order for orzo and hake was missing the orzo and garlic. The meal was incomplete and unenjoyable."
4.6 Technical Issues
797 tickets
Technical platform issues including website/app bugs and third-party login failures (Facebook, Google) prevent customers from accessing services and completing transactions. These issues directly impact conversion and user experience.
Common Issues
Website and app bugs preventing checkout, profile management, and subscription modifications
Facebook and Google login integration failures, requiring customers to use alternative authentication methods
4.7 Gifting
681 tickets
Gifting represents a new revenue opportunity with 681 inquiries. Customers are actively interested in purchasing gift subscriptions and vouchers/credits but lack a proper self-service mechanism, forcing them to contact support.
Customer Feedback
"I would love to gift a two-week subscription to my sister. Does UCOOK offer gift cards or subscriptions?"
"Is it possible to purchase credits or vouchers for UCOOK that I can give as gifts to friends?"

Recommendations

6.1 Address Management Enhancement Program
Conduct a comprehensive audit of address validation logic and integrate with updated geo-coding providers. The 9,845 tickets in this category represent 39% of themed issues. Implement real-time address validation with improved autocomplete that learns regional address formats. This should include unit testing against the most common failure patterns identified in support tickets and a user testing phase with 50+ customers experiencing the issue.
6.2 Self-Service Subscription Management
Build a comprehensive subscription management dashboard allowing customers to pause, cancel, skip deliveries, and modify preferences without contacting support. With 6,049 subscription management tickets and 5,665 peak month volume, the self-service opportunity is significant. The UI should be visible from the main dashboard with clear CTAs. Include options for temporary pauses (1-4 weeks) to differentiate from cancellation.
6.3 Delivery Experience Transformation
Implement automated delivery notifications at multiple stages (order confirmed, order ready for dispatch, out for delivery, delivered). Partner with logistics provider to enable delivery time window preferences and allow customers to reschedule or request specific delivery times. Create a customer-facing tracking portal integrated into the app showing real-time delivery status.
6.4 Billing Transparency Initiative
Establish monthly billing reconciliation processes and create a billing dashboard showing charge history, upcoming billings, and price breakdowns. Audit payment processing for errors causing duplicate charges or incorrect amounts. Implement proactive customer communication when charges differ from expectations, including automated refund processes for clear billing errors identified within 30 days of transaction.
6.5 Gifting & Growth Feature Launch
Build and launch a gift subscription feature allowing customers to purchase subscriptions as gifts with customizable delivery start dates and durations. Simultaneously introduce a digital voucher/credits system redeemable for subscriptions or meal credits. These 681 themed inquiries plus unquantified interest represent significant untapped revenue. Launch with targeted marketing to existing customers highlighting gifting options during peak seasons (holidays, birthdays).
