3.3

Meal Changes & Order Management

1,285 tickets (12.3% of all contacts) with a critical pattern: 1,228 changes happen during the billing window. This is a significant operational stress point and a key support driver. Customers are attempting to make changes right when billing locks menus.

Meal Changes During Billing Window (1,228 tickets) ★ ELEVATED
Sub-theme of Meal Changes & Order Management
EMAIL "I tried to change my meals but the system says 'billing in progress.' When can I swap my choices?"
WEB "The menu is locked for billing but I have a dietary restriction for next week. Can I still change it?"
WHATSAPP "I want to swap this meal but the app is blocking me. Is there a way around this?"
EMAIL "Your billing window is too early in the week. I need time to decide on meals."
Meal Changes After Billing (57 tickets)
Sub-theme of Meal Changes & Order Management
EMAIL "I realize I picked the wrong meal. Can I swap it even though billing has closed?"

Agent Pattern Alert: Support agents consistently offer ad-hoc manual meal swaps during the billing window, creating unscalable operational load. Recommend: Either extend the pre-billing menu window, implement a grace period post-billing, or develop a queue system for locked-window changes.

Implications for WhatsApp as Primary Channel:

  • Staffing & Infrastructure: WhatsApp now represents ~1/3 of all inbound. Ensure team has capacity and that WhatsApp Business API is robust—rate limits, message queues, and response SLA tracking are critical.
  • Automation & Chatbot: WhatsApp is increasingly used for urgent issues (delivery tracking, payment failures, pauses). Implement WhatsApp-native quick-reply buttons and bot escalation workflows to deflect routine queries.
  • Proactive Notifications: Leverage WhatsApp for pre-billing window alerts, delivery ETAs, and subscription milestones. Many of the meal-change and delivery-tracking tickets could be solved via WhatsApp notifications before they escalate to support.
