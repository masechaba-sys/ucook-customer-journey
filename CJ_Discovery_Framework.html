1 · Discover

  • Kickoff workshop
  • End-to-end journey mapping
  • Pain point identification
  • Team knowledge & intuition
  • Existing data & CX reports

2 · Validate

  • Customer interviews (JTBD)
  • Usability & session recordings
  • Quantitative surveys
  • Funnel & behavioural data
  • Support ticket analysis

3 · Prioritize

  • Confirm or refute assumptions
  • Rescore pain point backlog
  • Impact × effort matrix
  • Define success metrics (OKRs)
  • Agree what to build first

4 · Build

  • Solution design & prototyping
  • Engineering & development
  • QA & user testing
  • Ship & measure impact
  • Feed learnings into next loop
Core principles
Workshop outputs are hypotheses, not facts Validate the riskiest assumptions first Evidence overrides seniority Each Build cycle feeds the next Discover
