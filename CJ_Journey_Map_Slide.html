Pain Points — 16 Issues Across 5 Thematic Clusters
High Impact
Medium Impact
Cluster 1 Discovery & Pricing Clarity
Pricing transparency — customers don't understand what R800 means
Can't distinguish subscription vs on-demand products
Awareness campaigns break before plan selection
Don't know what they're buying before committing
Can't add single products to cart
Cluster 2 Onboarding & First-Box Friction
Promo codes don't auto-apply and aren't pulled through to cart
No proactive delivery comms — customers don't know when box arrives
Delivery areas not visible upfront
Delivery time not customisable
Cluster 3 Billing Transparency & Comms
Not reminded of upcoming billing — charges feel unexpected
Don't know when billed or on what cadence
Account creation email and next-step journey are unclear
Cluster 4 Pause & Reactivation Gaps
Pause journey is hidden — customers can't easily find how to pause
Paused customers not on mailer list receive zero communication
Cluster 5 Internal Knowledge Gaps
Limited demographic & behavioural insight beyond purchase data
On-demand conversion not well understood or optimised separately
